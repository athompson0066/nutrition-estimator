Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from MyFitnessPal or Cronometer?
Those apps capture YOUR potential clients and show them ads for other services. Your branded calculator keeps visitors on YOUR site, captures THEIR contact info, and positions YOU as the expert — not the app.
Do I need to be tech-savvy?
For the Done-For-You option, not at all. Fill out a form, we build it, you review. For the SaaS option, our dashboard is designed for nutritionists — no coding required.
Can I embed it on my existing website?
Yes! Your calculator can be embedded on Squarespace, Wix, WordPress, or any platform that supports iframes. Or host it standalone on your own domain.
What formulas do you use for macro calculations?
We use the Mifflin-St Jeor equation for BMR, multiplied by validated activity factors. Macro splits are based on ISSN and ACSM guidelines, adjustable for ketogenic, high-protein, or other protocols.
What happens after someone uses my calculator?
They see their personalized macros (calories, protein, carbs, fats, per-meal breakdown). To save their results, they enter their email — which goes directly to you for follow-up.
Is there a recurring fee?
For the Done-For-You and Premium plans, no — one payment and you own it forever. The SaaS plan is $49/month for ongoing platform access, lead management, and support.
